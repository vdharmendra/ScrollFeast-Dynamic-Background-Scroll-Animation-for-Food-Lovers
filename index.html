What It Is (Project Summary)

What is ScrollFeast?

ScrollFeast is a lightweight scroll animation framework that transforms static pages into engaging, visual stories — where background images change based on scroll position. Ideal for showcasing food, product journeys, or visual storytelling.

Key Features

Why You'll Love It:

  • Scroll-Reactive Backgrounds – Change scenes as users scroll
  • Simple to Implement – Just HTML, CSS, and optional JS
  • Fast & Lightweight – No bloat, no lag
  • Fully Responsive – Looks tasty on every device
  • Customizable – Plug in your own images, speed, and style
  • Perfect for Food & Lifestyle Brands

How It Works

As you scroll, ScrollFeast detects the scroll position and seamlessly updates the background to match the section. This creates a cinematic, layered storytelling effect with no heavy plugins.
